Today we make a our traditional food Besh!

  1. click to see Recipe

Recipe

  • Time: 2 hour
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
meat the more the better
onion 2
potato 4
carrot 4
flour 1 kg
water 10 litres

Step 1: pour water into the cauldron and throw the meat there

Step 2: and we will throw onions, potatoes and carrots into the soup with meat

Step 3: we make dough from flour and roll it out

Step 4: let's wait about 1-2 hours until the meat is ready

Step 5: after we take the meat from the cauldron and in the same soup we cook the dough we prepared earlier

Step 6: and all our food is ready
